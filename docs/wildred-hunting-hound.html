Wildred Hunting-Hound is lord of the County of Pellamstead. His seat is the Elm Fort. The only member of The Four Lords not of noble birth, he was elevated to the rank of Lord after his success in defeating a Fey invasion of his predecessor’s making. He is known for his cruelty and selfishness.

Though it is not an accurate (nor a polite) title, Hunting-Hound is known as The Baron to his subjects.

His wife is Edelgerd. He has sired no children.
